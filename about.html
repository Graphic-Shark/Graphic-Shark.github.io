About Graphic Shark

My name is Ethan Evans and I'm the Graphic Shark. Why a shark you may ask? Because sharks are driven towards their goals, once you see a shark headed your way, you know you’re toast. And that’s how I am with my design, once you set me on a task or project, I missile in on it and I don’t let up until the project is complete. I got my start doing animation for a demo program, then used the knowledge I gained from that area to transition over to graphic design. For eight years I worked hard to learn all I could about color theory, typography, and layout. After I felt confident in my skills in the physical world, I decided to focus on the digital one in 2014 and have loved it ever since.

I currently live in Utah in America and that’s where I plan on staying. The terrain is so diverse here that you can find just about any activity you want year around. The people here are amazing and same goes for those I’ve worked with. Not to say that others states aren’t great, but here in the heart of the mountains is where I feel most at home.

I hope you enjoy my work and if you have any comments or questions then please feel free to drop a line and I’ll be sure to take a bite. You can tap my tank at: graphicshark@gmail.com
