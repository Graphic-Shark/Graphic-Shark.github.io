RANLife Solar

RANLife Home Loans & Real Estate decided it would be advantageous to add a solar division to the company. However, rather than have it look similar to the rest of the company, they wanted it to have it's own branding and colors.

Tools Used

  • HTML/CSS
  • CSS ANIMATION
  • BOOTSTRAP
  • ANGULAR JS
  • PHOTOSHOP
  • ILLUSTRATOR

You can view the live site at: www.ranlifesolar.com

For this project I tried to do a bunch of minor animation effects to make the site a bit more fun. As you hover over some of the links you can see rays of sunshine pop out.
